ABOUT

Greenisis is a Tissue Culture lab based in Kerala, established in 2015 with a vision to promote agricultural production with the aid of technology. We seek to contribute to the agricultural sector by providing variety of plant tissue culture products in the domestic and foreign markets. We value our product quality and client relationships, and take great care in providing quality products to our customers. We take great care in upholding our product quality, which has helped us maintain a healthy base of satisfied clients, whom we ship products to, on a regular basis.
