ABOUT

Greenisis is a Tissue Culture Lab based in Alappuzha, Kerala. We at Greenisis intend to contribute to the agricultural yield of our country and hence we have forayed into the tissue culture business. Our focus is to make various varieties of plant tissue culture for domestic and export markets. We endeavor to produce quality products for customers to have healthy relationships. Greenisis has started business in 2015 with a vision. We strive to craft a world filled with opportunities and growth for the entire farming community by the application of life technologies. We aim to empower each and every farmer on the planet to produce and achieve more. We believe that customer satisfaction & long term relationships are the keys to success.
